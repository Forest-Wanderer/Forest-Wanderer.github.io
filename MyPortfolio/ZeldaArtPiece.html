Legend of Zelda Fanart

I made this piece on Procreate using various brushes. It is my take on Zelda from the upcoming Legend of Zelda game: Echoes of Wisdom. This is a unique new addition to my most beloved video game franchise. That being, the first mainline title where the player gets to control Zelda herself rather than the usual hero, Link. To commemorate that change, I decided to try my hand at drawing Zelda's new design!
