The Avatars

I made this piece on Procreate using various brushes. This piece is fanart for my favorite show of all time, Avatar the Last Airbender. This show pretty much is my entire personality and I wanted to commemorate the release of the live action version from Netflix. We don't have time for a discussion about that show right now. Anyway, this piece combines the visages of the four incarations of the avatar starting from Kyoshi.
